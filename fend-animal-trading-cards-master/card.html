Squirrel

Squirrels have been known to eat snakes and other rodents

  • Scientific Name: Syntheosciurus brochus
  • Average Length: 7 - 10 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 5 - 10 years
  • Habitat: Almost every habitat known

As squirrels are unable to digest cellulose, they have a protein, carbohydrate and fat rich diet. Their young are born naked, toothless and blind.
